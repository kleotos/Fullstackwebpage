All About Sweet potatoes

The best Vegetable there is

The sweet potato is a starchy,large root vegetable with a sweet taste. The are known to contain a relatively high nutritional value, Most notably the vitamin beta-carotene.Sweet potatoes have versatile uses for cooking. They can be Baked,steamed,fried,microwaved. They are frequently used in savory dishes,curries,soups,desserts,and much more.
